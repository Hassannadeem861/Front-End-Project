What is Uplift?
Uplift is your cutting-edge online platform, designed for self-discovery through open self-expression. Our platform offers a secure, confidential environment for sharing your feelings via chat, call, or video. Anonymity & Privacy is our top concern, and our Healers provide empathetic support. Find solace and new perspectives from your own space. Join Uplift today to rejuvenate your emotional well-being through compassionate, anonymous support.

Job Title: Supporter

About Us:
We are seeking empathetic and compassionate individuals who can provide support to those navigating emotional and mental challenges. Our platform aims to offer a safe space for individuals experiencing diverse emotional struggles, relationship issues, grief due to loss, or a sense of loneliness. We prioritize understanding, empathy, and non-judgmental support.

Responsibilities:

  • Engage with individuals seeking emotional support via our platform.
  • Offer empathetic listening and guidance to individuals experiencing emotional or mental distress.
  • Provide assistance to those coping with relationship issues, grief, loneliness, or other personal challenges.
  • Foster a safe and non-judgmental environment for open dialogue and support.

Qualifications:

  • Empathetic and compassionate nature.
  • Ability to actively listen and provide support without judgment.
  • Personal experience or understanding of emotional or mental distress.
  • Strong communication skills.
  • Respect for confidentiality and privacy.

Working Hours:

3 to 4 hours daily are compulsory, with the flexibility to extend hours based on personal availability.

Salary:

Compensation ranges from ₹4/min to ₹10/min, depending on the time devoted to supporting individuals on our platform.
